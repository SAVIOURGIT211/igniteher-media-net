Psychosocial Support and Mental Health Awareness

Displacement and trauma take a significant toll on mental health. Our programs aim to provide psychosocial support through mental health awareness campaigns, resource provision, and media content that promotes well-being. We work to raise awareness of the psychological impacts of displacement and offer safe spaces for individuals to heal and seek support

Youth Engagement and Talent Development

Our focus on youths empowerment helps to cultivate the next generation of leaders, advocates, and creatives. IgniteHer Media Network provides opportunities for youths, to showcase their talents and engage in meaningful activities that build their confidence, leadership skills, and sense of purpose

Media Advocacy
